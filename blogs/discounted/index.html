∫ Discounted differentiation and integration (#private)

Kernel:

  • Notably changes to differentiation and integration under a discount factor.

Suppose that you want to compute value of your saving under an inflation.

$$ \begin{align*} \frac{d_{\gamma} f(x)}{d_{\gamma} x} &= \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\gamma^{\Delta x} f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} \\ &= \gamma^{-x}\lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\gamma^{x + \Delta x} f(x + \Delta x) - \gamma^{x}f(x)}{\Delta x} \\ &= \gamma^{-x} \frac{d \gamma^{x} f(x)}{d x} \\ \end{align*} $$
