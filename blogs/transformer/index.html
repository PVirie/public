∫ Information leakage in transformers (#private)

Kernel:

A do and don't guideline when doing information manipulation with transformers.

  • Self-attention mask, full mask, and full causal mask work.
  • Non-self mask and n-step causal mask do not work.
  • All zero mask to sequence slot also causes information leakage in some implementations.

Transformer is a powerful architecture that has revolutionized the field of natural language processing. However, it is important to understand how information flows through the model, especially when it comes to masking. In this blog post, we will explore the different types of masks used in transformers and their impact on information leakage.
